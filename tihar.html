Tihar Festival

Tihar, also called Deepawali, is the festival of lights and colors. It lasts for five days and honors animals, wealth, and the bond between brothers and sisters.

Main Days of Tihar

  • Kaag Tihar (Crow Day)
  • Kukur Tihar (Dog Day)
  • Laxmi Puja (Goddess of Wealth)
  • Gobardhan Puja
  • Bhai Tika (Brother-Sister Bond)

How We Celebrate

People decorate homes with lights and rangoli, play Deusi-Bhailo, worship Laxmi, and brothers receive tika from sisters.
