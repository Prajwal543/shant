Chhath Festival

Chhath is a festival dedicated to the Sun God (Surya) and is mainly celebrated in the Terai and Mithila region. It includes fasting, holy bathing, and offering arghya during sunrise and sunset.

Main Rituals

  • Holy river worship
  • Fasting without water
  • Evening Arghya to setting sun
  • Morning Arghya to rising sun

Significance

It represents purity, discipline, and gratitude towards nature for sustaining life on Earth.
