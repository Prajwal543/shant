Dashain Festival

Dashain is the biggest and longest Hindu festival celebrated in Nepal. It lasts for 15 days and symbolizes the victory of good over evil. It is celebrated with great joy, family gatherings, animal sacrifices, and blessings from elders.

Main Days of Dashain

  • Ghatasthapana
  • Fulpati
  • Maha Ashtami
  • Maha Navami
  • Vijaya Dashami (Tika Day)

How We Celebrate

People buy new clothes, visit relatives, fly kites, play cards, build bamboo swings (ping), and receive tika and jamara from elders.
